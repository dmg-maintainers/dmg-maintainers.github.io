Atabak Elmi is a Senior Lecturer at the Deakin University in Mathematics and  
Optimisation. In 2014, he completed his PhD in Industrial Engineering at Dokuz  
Eylul University. He previously held teaching and research positions at  
Gaziantep University's Department of Industrial Engineering (2014-2017), and  
later at Monash University's School of Mathematics and Faculty of Engineering  
(2017-2020).  
  
Through his experience and knowledge of manufacturing, production, and  
transportation systems, he developed solutions to real-life planning and  
scheduling problems in these systems. A variety of Artificial Intelligence and  
Operations Research techniques as well as exact and approximate methods were  
applied to develop these methods. He has extensive teaching experience in  
operations research, project management and industry-oriented units.
